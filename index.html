Serving the Land

On the lush East side of the Big Island of Hawaiʻi, Natural Abundance transforms landscapes into thriving, productive ecosystems. We design and manage edible landscapes and organic farms that work with the land — not against it — creating abundance and resilience for generations.

Whether you have a backyard you'd love to fill with fruit trees or acreage ready to become a profitable working farm, we bring the expertise, the design, and the hands-on management to make it happen. Systems that work with nature require fewer inputs, less labor, and produce more reliably over time.

What We Do

Edible Landscape Design & Installation

Custom edible landscapes, fruit tree orchards, and tropical gardens tailored to your land. We design productive, beautiful systems that thrive in Hawaiʻi's unique microclimates.

Organic Farm Management & Maintenance

Ongoing care, soil building, and harvest management for productive organic systems. We keep your land healthy, efficient, and producing year-round.

Land Clearing & Site Preparation

Responsible clearing that preserves topsoil and prepares your land for planting. We work carefully to protect the existing ecosystem while making room for new growth.

Korean Natural Farming & Soil Building

Our Approach

Work With the Land

We read the landscape — slope, soil, water, wind — and design systems that thrive naturally. Working with natural processes means fewer purchased inputs, less labor, and better results.

Build Living Soil

Healthy soil is the foundation of everything. We build it through Korean Natural Farming inputs, compost, mulch, and care. Living soil grows healthier plants with less intervention.

Efficiency That Promotes Abundance

Every planting plan stacks functions — maximizing production while minimizing maintenance. Systems designed for efficiency that compounds over time, producing more with less input each season.
