WHAT WE DO

Our Services

From custom edible landscape design to ongoing farm management — productive systems that work with nature, not against it.

Tailored to Your Property

Every property presents its own conditions and possibilities. We assess your soil, microclimate, and goals to develop a plan specific to your land — one that maximizes production while keeping inputs low and maintenance manageable.

Edible Landscape Design & Installation

Custom edible landscapes, fruit tree orchards, and tropical gardens tailored to your land. We design productive, beautiful systems that thrive in Hawaiʻi's unique microclimates — from coastal properties to upland lots.

Organic Farm Management & Maintenance

Ongoing care, soil building, and harvest management for productive organic systems. Regular site visits, pruning, composting, and seasonal adjustments keep your land healthy, efficient, and producing year-round — with decreasing intervention as systems mature.

Land Clearing & Site Preparation

Responsible clearing that preserves topsoil and prepares your land for planting. We work carefully to protect the existing ecosystem while making room for new growth, managing invasive species and building access where needed.

Korean Natural Farming & Soil Building

As a Pure KNF certified practitioner and board member of the Pure KNF Foundation, Erik applies Korean Natural Farming principles to build living soil systems and grow nutrient-dense food without synthetic inputs. Biologically active soil means healthier plants, fewer purchased inputs, and lower operating costs — systems that are not only sustainable but more profitable over time.
